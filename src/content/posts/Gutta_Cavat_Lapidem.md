💧 Dripping water wears away a stone not with its strength, but with its constancy. 💦

> Gutta cavat lapidem non bis, sed sæpe cadendo; sic homo fit sapiens non bis, sed sæpe legendo.
> －Giordano Bruno, Il Candelaio
